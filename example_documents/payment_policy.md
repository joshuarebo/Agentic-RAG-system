# Corporate Payment Policy

## 1. Invoice Submission Requirements

All invoices submitted to the company must meet the following criteria:

- Invoice must include a valid Purchase Order (PO) number
- Invoice date must not be more than 90 days old
- Invoice must include the vendor's registered business name and address
- Invoice must clearly state the total amount due, including applicable taxes
- All line items must match the corresponding Purchase Order

## 2. Payment Terms

- Standard payment terms are Net 30 (payment due within 30 days of invoice receipt)
- Early payment discount: 2% discount if paid within 10 days (2/10 Net 30)
- Invoices above $50,000 require dual approval from department head and finance director
- International payments require additional compliance documentation

## 3. Compliance Requirements

- All vendors must have a valid W-9 (US) or W-8BEN (international) on file
- Invoices must comply with local tax regulations
- Duplicate invoice submissions will be flagged and rejected
- Invoices with discrepancies greater than 5% from the PO will be held for review

## 4. Late Payment Policy

- Payments made after the Net 30 deadline may incur a 1.5% monthly late fee
- The company reserves the right to negotiate extended payment terms for invoices above $100,000
- Vendors will be notified within 5 business days if an invoice cannot be processed

## 5. Approval Thresholds

| Amount | Approval Required |
|--------|-------------------|
| Under $5,000 | Department Manager |
| $5,000 - $50,000 | Department Head |
| Over $50,000 | Department Head + Finance Director |
| Over $100,000 | CFO Approval Required |
